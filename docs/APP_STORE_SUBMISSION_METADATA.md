# App Store Submission Metadata

Use this copy in App Store Connect and Google Play Console review notes for the initial mobile submission.

## Review Notes

SFLuv is operated by a nonprofit for community programs and real-world service coordination.

The app includes an Improver workflow area where eligible users can claim and complete real-world nonprofit tasks. Any visible SFLUV bounty or payout language refers only to compensation for verified real-world work administered by the nonprofit, such as approved community operations, field work, or service tasks.

SFLUV bounties are not offered for:

- downloading apps
- installing apps
- watching ads
- posting to social media
- rating or reviewing apps
- sharing marketing content
- increasing app engagement
- completing purely digital promotional activity

The app does not sell SFLUV through in-app purchase, does not exchange cryptocurrency for fiat inside the app, and does not require users to buy digital goods to participate in nonprofit-run workflows.

## Short Description

SFLuv is a nonprofit-run community wallet for sending SFLUV, redeeming perks, and coordinating verified real-world service tasks.

## Full Description Note

SFLuv helps community members use SFLUV, manage contacts, scan payment or reward QR codes, find participating locations, and coordinate nonprofit-run service workflows. Bounty rewards shown in the app are limited to verified real-world tasks administered by the SFLuv nonprofit. They are not rewards for app installs, app engagement, social-media actions, advertising views, ratings, reviews, or other digital promotional activity.

## Crypto / Rewards Disclosure

SFLUV is used in the app for community rewards and nonprofit program payouts. The app does not provide cryptocurrency exchange services, custodial brokerage services, speculative investment advice, or in-app purchases of cryptocurrency. Workflow bounties are tied to nonprofit-administered real-world service tasks and are reviewed through the app's backend workflow process before payout.
